CityBreeze Introduction:

Discover CityBreeze, a C++ developed weather terminal app that offers a seamless blend of functionality and simplicity for everyday use. CityBreeze utilizes the comprehensive OpenWeatherMap API to bring you up-to-the-minute weather data tailored for any city of your choice. Designed with a focus on user experience, it provides clear and concise weather insights, ensuring that you stay well-informed about the weather conditions that matter to you. Whether you're planning your work commute or gearing up for a weekend getaway, CityBreeze delivers reliable and accessible city forecasts right to your screen.
