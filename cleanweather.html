Clean Weather Introduction:

Introducing Clean Weather, a user-friendly weather app designed for everyone. Built on the reliable OpenWeatherMap API, CleanWeather brings you the latest weather updates in a straightforward and accessible way. Whether you're checking the forecast for your local area or a destination far away, this app provides clear weather information at your fingertips. Its simplicity and ease of use make it ideal for daily checks before heading out, ensuring you're always prepared for the day's weather. With CleanWeather, staying informed about the weather is effortless and efficient.
