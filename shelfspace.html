SHELFSPACE

ShelfSpace Introduction:


Explore ShelfSpace - a terminal app for book enthusiasts and librarians alike! ShelfSpace is an innovative platform that revolutionizes your reading experience by bringing your entire book collection into a virtual realm. With intuitive organization, and tracking tools, ShelfSpace transforms your scattered bookshelves into a cohesive, easily navigable library. Manage your personal reading list, and find your next favorite book with ease.


ShelfSpace code on GitHub

(ShelfSpace is developed using Java)


Add Books:

Add Books

With ShelfSpace, you can quickly add books to your digital collection by entering the title, author, ISBN, and the shelf where it's stored. The process is straightforward and efficient, allowing you to organize your books with ease. ShelfSpace is designed to make book management simple and accessible for everyone.


List Books:

List Books

Accessing your ShelfSpace inventory is a breeze, displaying a comprehensive list of your books with all the details including their location on your shelves. The inventory view is clear and user-friendly, enabling quick scans of your collection. It's the perfect way to keep tabs on your literary treasures and ensure every book is right where it should be.


Quit and Save Books:

Quit and Save Books

When you decide to exit ShelfSpace, rest assured that your entire inventory is meticulously saved to a file. This automatic save feature ensures that your book collection, along with its detailed organization by shelf location, is preserved and readily accessible for your next session. With ShelfSpace, closing the app is just a pause, not an end, to your literary management.
